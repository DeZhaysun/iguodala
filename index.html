Most Clutch Player of All Time
Andre Tyler Iguodala is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association. The swingman was the NBA Finals MVP in 2015, an NBA All-Star in 2012 and has been named to the NBA All-Defensive Team twice.
